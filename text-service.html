What is Text Annotation?

Text annotation is the process of labeling or tagging text data to make it understandable for machine learning models. By adding metadata, such as named entities, sentiment labels, parts of speech, or relationships between words, annotated text becomes the foundation for training natural language processing (NLP) models.
