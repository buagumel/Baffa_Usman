What is Video Annotation?

Video annotation is the process of labeling and tagging objects in videos to train artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) models for tasks like object detection, action recognition, and tracking. Unlike image annotation, video annotation requires frame-by-frame analysis to capture movement, interactions, and changing environments accurately.
