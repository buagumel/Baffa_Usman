Image Annotation and Its Importance

Image annotation is a crucial step in training machine learning models, especially for computer vision applications. It involves labeling or tagging images to provide meaningful information that helps AI systems recognize objects, patterns, and environments. This process is widely used in industries such as autonomous driving, healthcare, security, and e-commerce.
