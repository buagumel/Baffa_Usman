What is Audio Annotation?

Audio annotation is the process of labeling or tagging audio data—such as speech, music, or environmental sounds—with meaningful metadata to make it understandable for machine learning models. This involves tasks like transcribing speech-to-text, identifying speakers, tagging emotions or sentiments, classifying specific sounds, or marking timestamps. By adding these annotations, raw audio is transformed into structured data that trains AI systems to perform tasks like voice recognition, emotion analysis, sound detection, or automated transcription.
